This was my senior capstone project where we built an entire game from scratch, and this is what we ended up with. From the beginning the goal was to have a fully-fledged end product utilizing agile principles, and other software development techniques.

Main content for the game listed below.

  • Upgrade your character with a tech tree
  • Unlock abilities and weapons
  • Powerup your character with zombie drops
