Tic Tac Toe

Tic-tac toe is a game requiring you to match any three pieces across the board left/right, up/down, or diagonal.

  • You can choose to go first or let the computer go first
  • You can choose computer mode to be minimax or minimax with alphabeta pruning, your choice
  • The number of evaluated and expanded states as well as the move orders will be shown below
  • When ready to play a game, or play again press the "Star Game" button below the board
Take note of the difference between the number of evaluated and expanded states between both cpu modes.


Human player: First (X) Second (O)
CPU mode is: MiniMax MiniMax w/ Alpha-Beta Pruning
